About Us

Matic Cash (MATICC) is a peer-to-peer internet currency that enables instant, nearzero cost payments to anyone in the world. Matic Cash (MATICC) Is a decentralized finance currency on the Binance Smart Chain. A marketplace where people can buy, sell, and trade every day items using cryptocurrency. Matic Cash will be the primary currency, however we will integrate other tokens in the future

How to buy

01
01

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list.

02
02

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a «0x»

03

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for Matic Cash

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address to select Matic Cash. Set the slippage tolerance to 1 to 5%

04

View $MATICC and HODL

Swap BNB for Matic Cash. Now you need to add Matic Cash to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet to view your Matic Cash. Lastly HODL!
